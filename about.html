About Me

Heyo! I'm Olivia, a game programmer in the Orlando, Florida area. I'm currently finishing the game programmer technical track at FIEA, and will graduate with my MS later this year. I'll be ready to accept jobs / paid internships that begin in August, and am looking to find work near the Seattle, Washington area.

As a player, I love ability based shooters and puzzle focused horror and co-op games. As a developer, I love games that have highly systemic features, such as elemental status interactions, skill trees, and clean movement. It's quite fun to me to take game systems like these and break them down into easily digestible puzzle pieces. I also take great pride in ensuring my code has been designed to be clean to interact with, ensuring TDs and LDs that interface with my work have as many options as possible with the least possible amount of confusion.

I've hosted some of the work I'm proud of here, complete with short videos where I talk through some of the interesting parts of each project. Please take a look, and feel free to reach out to me with any questions or just to chat!
